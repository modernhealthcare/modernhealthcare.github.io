Colors

Modern Healthcare's primary colors are blue, red, orange and greys.

@cool-grey is our masthead color, but can also be found as a text color.

@warm-grey is used for eyebrows and/or label-like headings.

@crimson is the red color for our links.

@spice is the CTA (call-to-action) color for our buttons.

@sponsored is the base blue for sponsored content and is the color of any link in a sponsored content component.

@sponsored-tint is the background color for sponsored content component.

@black default text color.

@white default background color.
